Freezino's visual language at a glance: dark surfaces (gray-900 → gray-800 → gray-700), red `#DC2626` brand accent, gold `#FBBF24` for money/wins, green `#10B981` for confirmation, heavy emoji as iconography, Framer-Motion hover/tap scale, glowing colored shadows, rounded-xl cards with 1px gray-700 borders. Tone: Vegas-tacky on purpose, but anchored by "educational tool" reminders.
